About Website Safety Checker

What This Tool Does

The Website Safety Checker helps you identify potentially dangerous websites that might try to steal your information or install harmful software on your device. Our tool uses advanced artificial intelligence (AI) to analyze websites based on your answers to simple questions.

How It Works

When you answer our questions about a website, your responses are analyzed by six different AI models that have been trained to recognize dangerous patterns. These models work together to provide a final safety verdict.

Performance of our different AI models in detecting dangerous websites

Each model looks at different aspects of websites to determine if they might be:

  • Phishing sites that try to steal your passwords or personal information
  • Malware distributors that attempt to install harmful software
  • Scam websites that aim to trick you in various ways

Safety Tips

Protect Your Accounts
  • Use different passwords for different websites
  • Enable two-factor authentication when possible
  • Be suspicious of emails asking for your password
  • Regularly check your accounts for unusual activity
Browse Safely
  • Avoid clicking on suspicious links or pop-ups
  • Look for HTTPS in the address bar
  • Keep your browser and antivirus software up to date
  • Be cautious when downloading files from unknown sites

Who Can Use This Tool?

Anyone who wants to stay safe online! Whether you're a student, a working professional, or a casual internet user, the Website Safety Checker is designed to be simple and effective for everyone.
